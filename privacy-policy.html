Privacy Policy
At ChristineJoy Style Studio, we are committed to respecting your privacy and safeguarding your personal information. We believe that trust is the foundation of our customer relationships, and we work diligently to ensure your data is handled securely and transparently.

Information We Collect We collect only the information necessary to serve you better, including your name, contact details, and order preferences. This information is gathered when you make a booking, purchase products, or reach out to us for inquiries.

How Your Information Is Used Your personal data is used solely for processing appointments, fulfilling orders, responding to your questions, and improving our services. We never sell or share your information with third parties, except those directly involved in delivering your requested services (such as payment processors or delivery partners).

Data Security We use secure systems and best practices to protect your information. Access to your data is restricted to authorized personnel, and we regularly review our security measures to keep your information safe.

Your Rights and Choices You have the right to access, update, or request deletion of your personal data at any time. If you wish to make changes or have concerns about how your information is used, please contact us at contact@christinejoy-studio.com.

Cookies and Tracking Our website may use cookies to enhance your browsing experience and help us understand how our site is used. You can adjust your browser settings to manage cookies according to your preferences.

Policy Updates We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Any updates will be posted on this page, so please check periodically for the latest information.

Contact Us Your privacy matters to us. If you have any questions or need further clarification, please reach out by email at contact@christinejoy-studio.com or by phone at +63 927 730 1962.
