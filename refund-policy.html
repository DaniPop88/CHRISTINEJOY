Refund Policy
At ChristineJoy Style Studio, we strive to ensure your complete satisfaction with every purchase and service. If something isn’t right, we’re here to help and make the process straightforward.

Return & Refund Guidelines
  • Products from our Mini-Mart may be returned within 7 days of purchase with a valid receipt.
  • Returned items should be unopened, unused, and in their original packaging.
  • Refunds are processed to your original payment method once your return is approved.
  • Beauty and personal care services are non-refundable after they have been completed.
  • For quality concerns, please notify us within 24 hours of receiving a product or service.
How To Request a Refund If you need to request a refund or return, please contact us as soon as possible. You can reach us by email at contact@christinejoy-studio.com or call +63 927 730 1962. Provide your order details and reason for the request, and our team will guide you through the next steps.

Exceptions Certain items cannot be returned for hygiene reasons, such as opened cosmetics and personal care products. Customized packages and group bookings may have special terms; please review these at the time of purchase.

Processing Time Approved refunds are typically processed within 7 business days. You will receive confirmation once your refund has been completed.

We’re Here to Help Your satisfaction is important to us. If you have any questions about our refund policy or need assistance, please contact us by email or phone. We want every experience at ChristineJoy Style Studio to be positive and hassle-free.
